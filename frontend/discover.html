Things To Do

Explore the Highlands

Nuwara Eliya, perched at 1,868 metres above sea level, is a treasure trove of natural beauty, colonial history, and cultural richness waiting to be discovered.

🍃

Tea Estate Tours

Walk through lush green tea estates that produce some of the world's finest Ceylon tea. Watch skilled pluckers at work and taste freshly brewed single-estate teas.

2–3 hours 5 min from hotel
🏔️

Horton Plains National Park

Trek through misty cloud forests to World's End — a sheer cliff dropping 870 metres — and Baker's Falls. One of Sri Lanka's most dramatic landscapes.

Full day 35 km from hotel
🌸

Victoria Park

Stroll through colonial-era botanical gardens blooming year-round with exotic flowers including rhododendrons, magnolias, and rare orchids.

1–2 hours 10 min walk
🏇

Nuwara Eliya Race Track

The scenic 19th-century horse racing track at the heart of town is a reminder of the British colonial era and hosts races during the April season.

2 hours 15 min from hotel
🚂

Scenic Train Ride

Board one of the world's most scenic railways from Nanu Oya and journey through tea gardens, waterfalls, and mountain passes to Ella or Kandy.

3–5 hours 10 min to station
🎣

Fishing at Castlereagh

Cast a line at the reservoir amid gorgeous mountain scenery. Trout fishing is a popular activity in Nuwara Eliya's cool highland lakes.

3–4 hours 20 km from hotel
