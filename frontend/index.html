About Us

Eury Nature Cabana
In the Misty Highlands

Eury Nature Cabana is a peaceful, handcrafted wooden cabana retreat nestled in Sri Lanka's "Little England." We offer two exclusive cabanas — a spacious Main Cabana and a cozy Little Eury Nature Cabana — surrounded by misty mountains and lush tea gardens.

Each cabana is thoughtfully designed to give you privacy, comfort, and a deep connection with nature. Located just 12km from Nuwara Eliya and 4km from Ambewela Station, we are perfectly placed for highland adventures.

Our warm team ensures every moment is special — from guided tea estate walks to peaceful evenings around the BBQ under a starry highland sky. 🚫 No pets allowed.
