About Us

A Cozy Cabana Retreat
In the Misty Highlands

Cabana Nuwara Eliya is a peaceful, handcrafted wooden cabana retreat nestled in Sri Lanka's "Little England." We offer just three exclusive cabanas—one spacious family cabin and two cozy couple retreats—surrounded by misty mountains and lush tea gardens.

Each cabana is thoughtfully designed to give you privacy, comfort, and a deep connection with nature. Wake up to the aroma of fresh Ceylon tea delivered to your veranda as the morning mist rolls over the highlands.

Our warm and attentive team ensures every moment of your stay is truly special — from guided tea estate walks to peaceful evenings around the fire pit under a starry highland sky.
